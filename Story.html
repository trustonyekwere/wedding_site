Story

We met on a beautiful Tuesday. He came to market his wristwatch to me which I bought and unknown to me, a Seller-Customer relationship turned into something beautiful. After our first meeting and a few moments, He asked me out several times because he truly loved me from his heart, But as a young and Naive girl, who was hurt previously turned him down severally as I stopped believing in love. But finally I said yes to him... and for once I never regretted our choice of been together...
